What is Soccer?

Two teams of eleven players each compete in the exciting and dynamic sport of soccer, which is also referred to as football in many areas of the world. Soccer is the most popular sport in the world because it is easy to play and requires little equipment, making it suitable for players of all ages and skill levels. With a long history and a large international following, soccer unites people from every walk of life, transcending cultural and linguistic divides as it develops a love for the game.

When is Soccer Played?

  • Year-Round Leagues
    • Top leagues like the Premier League and La Liga play from August to May.
    • Schedules vary slightly between countries but typically cover most of the year.
  • International Tournaments
    • The World Cup is held every four years, showcasing national teams globally.
    • Continental tournaments like the Euro Cup and Copa America occur every few years.
  • Friendly Matches and Pre-Season
    • Friendly games allow teams to prepare and try new strategies before official seasons.
    • Clubs often travel internationally for exhibition matches in the off-season.
  • Local and Amateur Games
    • Played throughout the year, often following weather or school calendars.
    • Informal games are common in parks and community fields year-round.
